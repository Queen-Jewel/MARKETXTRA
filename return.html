1. Return Eligibility
To be eligible for a return, please ensure that:
- The item was purchased within the last 30 days.
- The item is in its original packaging.
- The item is unused and in the same condition as when you received it.
- Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) is provided.
Certain items are not eligible for return, including:
- Perishable goods (e.g., food, flowers)
- Personalized items
- Intimate or sanitary goods
- Hazardous materials or flammable liquids and gases

2. Return Process
Step 1: Initiate a Return
- Contact our customer service team at [Your Contact Email] or [Your Contact Phone Number] to initiate a return. Provide your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason for the return.
Step 2: Prepare the Package
- Pack the item securely in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and documentation.
- Include the proof of purchase and a note with your contact information and reason for return.
Step 3: Ship the Package
- You will be responsible for the cost of return shipping. We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance.

3. Refunds
Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item and notify you of the status of your refund. If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days. Please note that it may take additional time for your bank or Credit Card Company to process and post the refund.

4. Exchanges

5. Non-Returnable Items
